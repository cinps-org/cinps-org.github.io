Transforming Aspirations into Healthcare Careers

Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science (CINPS) is a professionally governed paramedical institute committed to delivering structured, skill-driven, and career-oriented healthcare education. We prepare students for responsible roles in hospitals, diagnostic centers, and emergency services.

We operate with a clear academic vision: to transform aspiring learners into competent professionals through disciplined training systems, practical exposure, and measurable performance standards.

🏛️ Structured Academic System

Education at CINPS extends beyond classroom instruction. Our framework strictly integrates real-world applicability with academic rigor:

  • Concept-based theoretical learning modules
  • Procedure-oriented practical training in modern labs
  • Continuous skill assessment and benchmarking
  • Professional workplace behavior monitoring
  • Outcome-based evaluation methods

🏥 100% Internship Assurance

Bridge the gap between theory and practice.

  • Real-time clinical exposure in partner hospitals
  • Direct application of concepts in practical settings
  • Focus on workplace discipline and clinical documentation

💼 100% Placement Assistance

Dedicated support for launching your career.

  • One-on-one career counseling and guidance
  • Intensive mock interview preparation sessions
  • Professional resume and portfolio development
