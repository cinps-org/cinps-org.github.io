DMLT Course Details 2026

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT) is one of the most highly sought-after paramedical courses following Higher Secondary (HS). It provides a robust foundation for building a successful career in hospitals, diagnostic labs, and modern healthcare centers.

🎓 Course Overview

  • Eligibility: Students who have passed HS (10+2) in any stream.
  • Duration: 2 Years + Mandatory Clinical Internship.

👨‍🏫 Expert Faculty & Training

Our curriculum balances rigorous academics with practical mastery. Students receive comprehensive theory instruction from specialists like Doyel Ghosh, combined with intensive, hands-on DMLT practical training led by Rupam Maji, ensuring you are fully prepared for a fast-paced laboratory environment.

🏥 Real-World Clinical Internships

Gain Practical Experience: We guarantee unparalleled field exposure. CINPS students complete their clinical internships across most government hospitals in the Birbhum district, working directly with real patient samples alongside seasoned medical professionals.

💼 Career Scope & Placements

The demand for skilled lab technicians is growing rapidly. Career paths include:

  • Government and Private Hospital Laboratories
  • Advanced Diagnostic Centers and Pathology Labs
  • Blood Banks and Research Facilities

Our graduates have a strong track record of success, with former DMLT students securing competitive positions at top institutions across the region, including Gitanjali Hospital in Bardhaman.

💰 Expected Salary

Starting Salary: ₹10,000 – ₹25,000/month (varies based on technical proficiency, location, and the hiring institution).
