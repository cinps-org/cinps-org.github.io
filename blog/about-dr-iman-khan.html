Dr. Iman Khan

Founder & Managing Director, CINPS

Dr. Iman Khan is the visionary Founder and Managing Director of the Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science (CINPS). He is an education entrepreneur, institute leader, and independent researcher committed to expanding access to practical, affordable, and career-oriented paramedical education.

🎓 Educational & Professional Background

Dr. Khan holds a Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in History and has been awarded an honorary doctorate for his significant contribution to education and social development.

🏆 Achievements

  • Founder & Managing Director of CINPS (Multi-branch paramedical institute)
  • Best Educationalist Award – Bangalore Group of Institutions
  • Honorary Doctorate for contribution to education
  • Research author with presence on SSRN, Google Scholar, and Zenodo

🚀 Entrepreneurial Journey

Dr. Khan started his journey with multiple ventures and faced early challenges in the startup ecosystem. Through persistence, a problem-solving mindset, and continuous learning, he successfully established CINPS, which is now helping students build secure, high-value careers in the healthcare sector.

📚 Research & Academic Contributions

Alongside his institutional leadership, Dr. Iman Khan is actively involved in academic research focusing on language, cognition, and institutional development.

📝 Published Research Paper

"Why Certain Concepts Appear Late in Human Language: A Cognitive–Institutional Perspective"

  • Author: Iman Khan
  • Published: 2026
  • Availability: SSRN, Google Scholar, and Zenodo

This research explores why some concepts emerge late in language despite being cognitively understood, highlighting the role of social relevance, communication patterns, and institutional influence.

🌍 Vision

To architect a highly skilled healthcare workforce and make paramedical education broadly accessible, highly affordable, and practical-oriented for students across rural and urban regions.

His broader vision includes building institutions that combine skill education, ethical leadership, practical healthcare training, and intellectual contribution through ongoing research.

“Focus strictly on skill development, practical knowledge, and discipline. A strong foundation in education will open long-term career opportunities.”
– Dr. Iman Khan
