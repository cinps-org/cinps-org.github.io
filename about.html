About CINPS

Chandidas Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science (CINPS) is dedicated to providing quality education in nursing and paramedical sciences. Our focus is on academic excellence, ethical values, and practical skill development.

CINPS aims to prepare students for real-world healthcare environments through structured learning, hands-on training, and continuous academic guidance.
