Institution Overview

Established in 2017, Chandidas Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science (CINPS) is committed to delivering skill-focused healthcare education with structured practical exposure. The institute aims to produce disciplined, ethical and industry-ready paramedical professionals.

Academic Approach

CINPS integrates concept-based theory with procedure-oriented practical sessions, continuous skill assessment, and outcome-based evaluation. Simulation labs, real-time clinical exposure, and mentor-driven batches ensure workplace readiness.

  • Small batch sizes & mentor supervision
  • Simulation labs for OT, ICU & DMLT
  • Structured internships & placement assistance
