About CINPS

Chandidas Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science (CINPS) is dedicated to shaping the future of healthcare by providing world-class education, hands-on training, and a strong foundation in medical sciences. Established with a vision to bridge the gap between the growing healthcare demand and skilled professionals, CINPS strives for excellence in paramedical education and innovation in the medical field.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values needed to excel in the healthcare industry. Through a perfect blend of theory and practical training, we prepare our graduates to tackle real-world medical challenges with confidence and expertise.

Our Vision

To become a premier institution in paramedical education, recognized for academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and producing highly skilled healthcare professionals who uphold the highest standards of patient care and ethics.

Why Choose Us?

  • Industry-centric curriculum aligned with modern medical practices
  • State-of-the-art facilities, advanced labs and simulation tools
  • Expert faculty with real healthcare experience
  • Hands-on hospital internships for practical exposure
  • Career support and placement assistance

Message from the Founder

“True education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it is about transforming lives, igniting passion, and inspiring a commitment to serve humanity.” – Dr. Iman Khan, Founder & MD

CINPS aims to prepare students for real-world healthcare environments through structured learning, hands-on training, and continuous academic guidance.
