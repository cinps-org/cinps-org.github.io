Skill-Focused Healthcare Education with Structured Practical Exposure

Chandidas Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science (CINPS) is committed to developing competent, ethical and industry-ready healthcare professionals.

Institutional Commitment

CINPS was established with a clear mission to bridge the gap between theoretical medical education and real-world clinical practice. Our structured academic system ensures students receive disciplined classroom instruction combined with guided hospital-based practical exposure.

Academic Discipline

Structured syllabus, scheduled classes, continuous evaluation and academic monitoring.

Practical Training

Hands-on laboratory sessions and supervised hospital internships.

Ethical Foundation

Strong emphasis on patient safety, professional conduct and healthcare ethics.

Career Guidance

Guidance, mentorship and preparation for real clinical environments.
