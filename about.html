Institution Overview

Chandidas Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science (CINPS) was founded in 2017 with the vision of creating a skilled workforce for India's healthcare system. We focus exclusively on diploma and certificate paramedical courses, aligning classroom theory with intensive hands-on practice and 100% internship assurance for eligible students.

Academic Approach

CINPS follows a structured academic system that mixes concept-based theory with procedure-oriented practical sessions, continuous skill assessment, and outcome-based evaluation. Practical exposure, simulated labs, and compulsory internships (for eligible students) ensure workplace readiness.

  • Small batch sizes & mentor-driven training
  • Simulation-based labs for clinical procedures
  • Regular performance feedback & skill logs
  • Dedicated placement assistance and employer coordination
