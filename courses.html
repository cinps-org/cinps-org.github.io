Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science

Transforming learners into confident healthcare professionals

At our institute, we blend rigorous academics with hands-on clinical training so graduates leave job-ready, confident, and compassionate. Industry-aligned syllabi, experienced faculty, realistic simulation labs, and strong hospital tie-ups give students the practical edge employers look for.

Signature Programs

Diploma in Operation Theatre Technology (DOTT)

Duration: 2 years (+ 6 months internship)

A career-focused program for perioperative care and surgical support. Students master sterile technique, instrument handling, anaesthesia assistance, surgical patient preparation and monitoring, and emergency theatre protocols.

Eligibility: 10+2 (Science/Arts) or equivalent.

Key modules:

  • Surgical anatomy & instruments
  • Anaesthesia fundamentals
  • Asepsis & infection control
  • Perioperative patient care
  • Emergency theatre procedures

Clinical training: Six-month internship in partnered government/private hospitals.

Career paths: OT technician, surgical supply coordination, theatre supervision, advanced anaesthesia technology roles.

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)

Duration: 2 years (+ 6 months internship)

Designed for students passionate about diagnostic medicine, this program covers clinical biochemistry, microbiology, pathology, hematology, and laboratory quality assurance.

Eligibility: 10+2 (Science preferred; Arts considered).

Key modules:

  • Clinical biochemistry
  • Medical microbiology
  • Histopathology
  • Blood banking
  • Laboratory instrumentation & automation

Clinical training: Six months in accredited diagnostic laboratories.

Career paths: Lab technologist, phlebotomy specialist, quality control, research assistant, higher studies (BSc DMLT).

Intensive Care Unit Technician

Duration: 2 years (+ 6 months internship)

An advanced program focused on critical care support including ventilators, monitoring systems, emergency response, and ICU protocols.

Eligibility: 10+2 (Science/Arts).

Key modules:

  • Mechanical ventilation
  • Hemodynamic monitoring
  • Critical care pharmacology
  • Infection prevention in ICU

Clinical training: Hands-on ICU rotations during internship.

Career paths: ICU technician, critical care support staff, ER and ambulance support.

Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)

Duration: 2 years (+ 6 months internship)

Designed for frontline lifesavers. Students learn pre-hospital care, trauma management, emergency response, and ambulance transport protocols.

Eligibility: 10+2 (Science/Arts).

Key modules:

  • Pre-hospital trauma life support
  • CPR & advanced first aid
  • Ambulance operations
  • Disaster response

Clinical training: Ambulance drills and ER internship.

Career paths: EMT in ambulance services, disaster-response teams, hospital emergency units.

Intensive Care Unit Technician

Duration: 1 year (+ 6 months internship)

A compact, high-impact diploma for learners seeking focused ICU skills in a shorter timeframe. Ideal for career changers and motivated 10th-pass candidates.

Eligibility: 10th pass (Science/Arts).

Key modules: Core ICU skills, emergency response, patient monitoring fundamentals.

Career paths: ICU support roles and step-down units; pathway to advanced certifications.

Registered Medical Practitioner (RMP / DRMP)

Duration: 1 year

A practical diploma for community-level medical service providers, designed for candidates with prior field experience or basic clinical exposure.

Eligibility: 10th pass or 2–3 years medical field experience (where applicable).

Key modules: Basic clinical assessment, common ailment management, legal & ethical boundaries, medical record keeping.

Community Medical Service & Essential Drugs (CMS & ED)

Duration: 18–24 months

Designed to strengthen primary healthcare delivery in rural and underserved communities. Focuses on basic allopathic care, preventive health education, and essential drug management.

Eligibility: 10th pass.

Career paths: Rural primary care worker, NGO health programs, community health outreach.
