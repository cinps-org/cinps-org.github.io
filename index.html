Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science (CINPS)

Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science (CINPS) is a professionally governed paramedical institute committed to delivering structured, skill-driven, and career-oriented healthcare education. The institute focuses on diploma and certificate programs designed to prepare students for responsible roles in hospitals, diagnostic centers, emergency services, operation theatres, and intensive care environments.

CINPS operates with a clear academic vision: to transform aspiring healthcare learners into competent professionals through disciplined training systems, practical exposure, and measurable performance standards. Every academic activity is aligned with real healthcare industry requirements to ensure students graduate with confidence and operational readiness.

Structured & Career-Focused Academic System

At CINPS, education is not limited to classroom instruction. The academic framework integrates:

  • Concept-based theoretical learning
  • Procedure-oriented practical training
  • Continuous skill assessment
  • Professional behavior monitoring
  • Outcome-based evaluation methods

100% Internship Assurance

Practical training is a mandatory component of professional qualification. CINPS provides 100% internship opportunities to eligible students as part of its structured academic system.

  • Gain real-time clinical exposure
  • Apply theoretical concepts in practical settings
  • Develop confidence in equipment handling
  • Understand workplace discipline and documentation standards
  • Strengthen patient interaction skills

100% Job Placement Assistance

CINPS supports students beyond academic completion by offering job placement assistance and career development services for qualified candidates.

  • Career counseling and professional guidance
  • Interview preparation sessions
  • Resume development assistance
  • Professional communication training
  • Coordination with healthcare employers

Comprehensive Student Support System

Academic Support

  • Doubt-clearing sessions
  • Structured revision programs
  • Performance feedback discussions
  • Examination preparation assistance

Professional Development Support

  • Communication skill enhancement
  • Workplace behavior training
  • Documentation accuracy guidance
  • Time management coaching

A Trusted Choice for Students and Parents

Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science maintains a disciplined, transparent, and professionally managed academic environment. With structured programs, assured internships, and dedicated placement assistance, CINPS builds capable, ethical, and employment-ready paramedical professionals.
