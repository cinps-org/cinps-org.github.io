About CINPS

Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science (CINPS) is a professionally managed healthcare education institute dedicated to developing skilled, disciplined, and employment-ready paramedical professionals. The institution focuses on structured diploma and certificate programs designed to meet the practical demands of hospitals, diagnostic centers, emergency units, operation theatres, and intensive care settings.

With a strong academic framework and a career-oriented approach, CINPS integrates theoretical knowledge, practical skill development, and professional discipline into a unified training system. The objective is to produce competent healthcare professionals who are technically sound, ethically responsible, and confident in real clinical environments.

A Distinctive Approach to Paramedical Education

  • Skill-Centric Training System: Hands-on competency development including equipment handling, patient assistance, infection control, documentation, and emergency response.
  • Outcome-Based Academic Evaluation: Certification reflects verified competence through written exams, practical assessments, viva, and behavioral monitoring.
  • Ethical & Professional Discipline: Accountability, teamwork, punctuality, and professional ethics are core institutional values.
  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Programs aligned with modern medical technologies and healthcare operational standards.
