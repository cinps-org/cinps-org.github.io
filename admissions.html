Welcome to CINPS Admissions

We invite motivated learners who aspire to build a meaningful and stable career in the healthcare sector. Admissions are granted through a transparent and merit-focused process to ensure focused training and personalized mentorship.

Selection Process

1. Eligibility Assessment

  • Candidates must meet the minimum educational qualification specified for the chosen course.
  • Eligibility varies by program (10th / 10+2 with Science or Arts, as applicable).

2. Application & Verification

  • Submission of completed application form.
  • Verification of academic certificates, identity proof, and photographs.
  • Review of prior medical experience (if applicable).

3. Counseling & Guidance

  • One-to-one counseling to explain course structure, clinical training, and internships.
  • Parents and guardians are welcome to participate.

4. Seat Allotment

  • Seats allotted based on eligibility and availability.
  • Admission confirmed after completion of formalities and fee payment.
  • Official admission confirmation receipt issued.

⭐ Why Apply Early?

  • Limited seats for quality-focused batches
  • Priority access to preferred courses
  • Early academic orientation
  • Better planning for internships

📌 Important Note

Admission at Chandidas Institute of Nursing and Paramedical Science is conducted in a fair, ethical, and student-centric manner. We do not encourage shortcuts—only genuine eligibility, commitment, and passion for healthcare.
