How to Apply

  1. Prepare academic documents (mark sheets, ID proof, recent photo).
  2. Complete the online or in-person application form.
  3. Attend the admission interview or skill assessment (dates announced on the website).
  4. Secure your seat by paying the prescribed admission fee.
